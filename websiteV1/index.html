WhatPiece

This is a site I made for Gear optimization for players of World of Warcraft. It's no longer up to date because of changes in the game. When current This site could help players plan out their gear purchases in the beginning weeks of a season. The problem facing players concerning which pieces to buy and when is modified version of the Knapsack problem. Site was made entirely with PHP. and the algorithm for figuring out what pieces of gear to buy ended up being alot more complicated than I first thought. Check out result.php on the GitHub if you want to see it.
